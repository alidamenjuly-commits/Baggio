Il buon gusto
non fa rumore.

Menswear held together by a quiet standard.

Baggio was built on a single conviction — that a wardrobe made of fewer, better pieces will always outdress one made of many. The polos are knitted with weight. The linens are cut for how a man actually stands. Every colour, every cuff, every hem is chosen because it earns its place, not because it fills the rack.

Come to the bottega on Malla street, or order to your door anywhere in Lebanon. Either way, the standard travels with the parcel.
